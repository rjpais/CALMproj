Description of the model "Model"

Annotation

Logical network model of the regulation cell-cell and focal adhesion dynamics.

Model is composed by a total of 51 nodes and 136 interactions:
-8 inputs (signals from ECM and neighbouring cells).
- 2 outputs (cell-cell adhesion strength and focal adhesion dynamics).
- 41 internal nodes (membrane proteins, signalling cascade proteins, transcription factors genes and micro RNA).

Network model includes:
1) Phospho-regulation of E-cadherin/catenins complex;
2) Transcriptional regulation of E-cadherin by SNAIL and ZEB;
3) Growth factors & integrin signalling crosstalk;
4) Notch, TGFb & growth factor signalling crosstalk;
5) Hippo & Wnt/beta-catenin signalling crosstalk.
6) STAT3 and Hypoxia Signalling.
